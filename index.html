About Me Software · Networks · Electronics — three disciplines, one engineer.

Get to know me!

I'm an 8th-semester IT Engineering student at Escuela Politécnica Nacional (EPN), Quito. I combine three areas — Full-Stack Development, Networking, and Electronics/IoT — to build end-to-end technology solutions.

I enjoy building web applications with JavaScript, React and Node.js, designing REST APIs, and working with databases. I care about clean code, open source, and shipping things that actually work.

On the infrastructure side I configure routers, switches, and Linux servers. For IoT I prototype with Arduino, C/C++, and Python, connecting physical sensors to cloud dashboards. Actively looking for an internship to apply these skills in real environments.
