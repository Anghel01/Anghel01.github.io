About Me Software · Networks · Electronics — three disciplines, one engineer.

Get to know me!

I'm an 8th-semester IT Engineering student at Escuela Politécnica Nacional (EPN), Quito. I combine three areas — Full-Stack Development, Networking, and Electronics/IoT — to build end-to-end technology solutions.

I enjoy designing and shipping web and mobile applications, working with JavaScript, React, and Node.js, and architecting REST APIs. I'm also passionate about database modeling, clean code, and open source.

On the infrastructure side I configure routers, switches, and Linux servers. For IoT I prototype with Arduino, C/C++, and Python, connecting physical sensors to cloud dashboards. I'm actively looking for an internship to apply these skills in real-world environments.
